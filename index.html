About Me

My name is Isaac Beverly, and I am from Durham, NC. I am a Christian, a husband of an amazing wife, and father of two children. I have a strong interest in programming, and my goal is to become a software engineer and to potentially learn cybersecurity as well.

My story is one that has required grit and determination. I am a graduate from The University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill with two separate degrees in Dramatic Arts and Communication with a focus in Performance Studies. I have also be a personal trainer an actor, and an associate pastor in my life, and I currently have worked my way up to be a Captain (Manager) at Chick-fil-A. Chick-fil-A has developed my leadership skills, interpersonal skills, and my work ethic, and it is in this context that I have also become a self-taught programmer with a great desire to serve others.
